Pre-Flight Inspection Registration

Before the DJI Data Manager can record flight data there needs to be a record of the flight and its inpection. Complete the questions below to initaite a flight. Avoiding drone flight accidents can be as simple as identifying issues with the drone pre-flight. All questions are required.


Flight Name

Enter a name for your flight here. Make it memorable so you can easily retrieve flight postflight feedback. An example of a name would be "First flight at cherry hill park." You do not need to make it too long or include a date.

Battery Charges

The charge of the drone's battery can be found at the tail end of the drone on the removable battery. The controller's charge can be found on the face of the controller at the bottom. It is recommended that a drone operator has full charge in both the drone and controller before taking flight.

Propeller Conditions

All propeller positions are relative to a top-down forward facing view of the DJI Phantom. It is reccomended that all cracked propellers are replaced before additional flights.
